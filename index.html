Assignment 2

This was a surprisingly difficult assignment for me. There felt like so many options for how to do everything that I spent a lot of time trying to figure out the "best" one. I think that the hardest part for me was getting the twits lined up so there were only 3 and that they did not go past a certain size. At first I used a flex box, but I could not figure out how to have a max of 3 twits in a row. So I ended up using grid and setting it to 3 columns then when the page was shrunk down to 1000px, it would make 2 columns.

The most useful resource (aside from lectures) was https://www.w3schools.com/css. They had tons of info and various methods I could use to accomplish tasks. It was very helpful for the navbar as I couldn't figure out how to center the text in the bar.
